Commonly Asked Questions

Why is the sky blue?

The sky appears blue because of the way Earth's atmosphere scatters sunlight.

Why do birds sing in the morning?

Birds sing to communicate, attract mates, and mark their territory.

How does Wi-Fi work?

Wi-Fi transmits data using radio waves, allowing devices to connect to the internet wirelessly.

What causes seasons on Earth?

Earth's tilt on its axis and its orbit around the Sun cause the changing seasons.

Why do we dream?

Dreams may help process emotions, memories, and subconscious thoughts.

How does a camera work?

A camera captures light using a lens and records it digitally or on film.
